ABOUT ARUN RAJPUT

A New Generation Of Leadership

Arun Rajput represents a progressive vision built on humanity, opportunity, and development for ordinary people.

While working in Mumbai, Arun witnessed the everyday struggles faced by families, youth, workers, and vulnerable communities searching for opportunities in rapidly growing cities.

During this period, he became actively involved in helping people locally and feeding stray dogs, believing that compassion and humanity should always come before power.

These experiences shaped his belief that leadership should focus on solving real problems — housing, healthcare, education, youth opportunities, and transparent governance.

“Politics should not divide people. It should improve lives.”
